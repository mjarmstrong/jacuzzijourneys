At Jacuzzi Journey, we aim to offer unforgettable outdoor experiences combining the serenity of nature with luxurious hot tub and camping relaxation.

We are committed to eco-friendly adventures that rejuvenate the mind, body, and spirit, while providing exceptional customer service and respecting the environment. Our goal is to inspire a love for the outdoors and create lasting memories for every guest.

What is a hot tug experience?

Come explore the Bow Valley on a gorgeous boat, from a cozy hot tub — marveling at Ghost Lake, the Bow River and the rocky mountains. We offer Alberta's premier Hot Tug Boat rental experience, complete with Bluetooth speakers, easy joystick navigation, and plenty of space for food and drinks. We’re here to help you make great memories and celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, and fun times with friends and family.
