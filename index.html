Imagine soaking in a wood-fired hot tub while cruising along scenic waterways, surrounded by stunning landscapes and accompanied by good company. That's the essence of a hot tug adventure with us.

Our unique offerings cater to individuals, couples, families, and groups looking for a one-of-a-kind experience. Whether it's a leisurely cruise to unwind and take in the views or a lively celebration with friends, our hot tugs provide the perfect setting.

Take a look at our unique offerings

Hot Tug Experiences

Enjoy unforgettable adventures on the water with your one-of-a-kind portable hot tubs. Guests can soak in the warm waters while cruising along scenic waterways, taking in breathtaking views, and enjoying the company of friends or family.

Camping Retreats

Escape the hustle and bustle of city life with immersive camping retreats in picturesque locations. Set up camp in serene natural settings, providing guests with a chance to reconnect with nature and unwind under the stars.

Tree Tent Accommodations

Elevate the camping experience with unique tree tents suspended above the ground. These innovative accommodations offer comfort and adventure, allowing guests to sleep amidst the trees while enjoying stunning views and the soothing sounds of nature.

Guided Adventures

Offer guided tours and experiences led by experienced outdoor enthusiasts. Whether it's exploring hidden gems in the wilderness, embarking on scenic hikes, or discovering local wildlife, guided adventures provide guests with memorable experiences and valuable insights into the natural world.
