About us

At Jacuzzi Journey, our mission is to provide unforgettable outdoor experiences that blend the serenity of nature with the luxury of hot tug and camping relaxation.

We are dedicated to creating unique, eco-friendly adventures that rejuvenate the mind, body, and spirit, while ensuring exceptional customer service and a deep respect for the environment. Our goal is to inspire a love for the outdoors and create lasting memories for every guest.

Join us at Jacuzzi Journey for an unforgettable experience where relaxation meets adventure on the water. Get ready to soak, camp, and create lasting memories with us!

Powered by eco-friendly wood-fired heaters, our hot tugs offer a sustainable way to enjoy the water while minimizing our environmental footprint. Safety and comfort are our top priorities, ensuring that every adventure with us is not only memorable but also secure.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When can I rent a Hot Tub Boat?

Right now! We are open all year, rain or shine.

How hot is the water in your Hot Tub Boats?

The "Feds" say the hottest, maximum temperature for a hot tub is 104 degrees. So, that’s where we keep it!

Does the hot tub stay hot while boating?

Yes, the Hot Tub Boat is equipped with a small, diesel fired boiler to maintain the temperature of your choice.

Who drives the boat?

You, your friend, your Aunt, your best friend's cousin's girlfriend - doesn't matter to us, as long as they are 18 and above!

Can I bring alcohol during my Hot Tub Boat rental?

Unfortunately, no. We know this may be disappointing, however, please take a moment and look at it from our liability perspective. Besides, haven’t we always been told that drinking and hot tubs don’t mix?
